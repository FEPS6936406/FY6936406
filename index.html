Abstract

  For many years, innovation has been the foundation to Formula One motor racing. Over
 the past few decades,Formula One has had innovations that had revolutionised and shaped the sport for decades to come and have been implemented ever since.
 However, some innovations have been banned due to safety, the sole dominance of one team or cost effectiveness. This presentation will explain F1’s ground-breaking innovations that were later
 banned.
